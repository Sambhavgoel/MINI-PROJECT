ABOUT RAIN RADAR

Rain Radar is a web app which has a Machine Learning Model running at the back. The purpose of developing this app is to protect whether it will be rainy day or not. This model is based on the Rain Prediction in India Dataset. India has an average annual rainfall of around 125 cm of rain (890mm). Due to the country's very varied geography, including relief or topography, distance from the sea, etc.,
The coastal regions along the Western Ghats and the northeastern states, receive higher rainfall, ranging from 2,500 to 5,000 milimeters(98 to 197 inches)annually. India has the climate of Tropical monsoon due to its peculiar position in the Asian Continent. Indian Climate is mostly characterized by Wet and Dry seasons.
